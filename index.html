Trust infrastructure for organizations that must prove how their information was built.

Narratio is a SaaS + API-first platform that converts data and content into traceable, explainable, and auditable evidence. It integrates as a horizontal layer within existing enterprise stacks — without migrations, without tooling replacement.

The platform does not operate in true/false. It delivers a structured TrustScore, an explainable Evidence Graph, and audit-ready metadata — enabling organizations to demonstrate informational provenance when challenged by regulators, auditors, or the public.

Narratio is enterprise-first, production-deployed, and operating in high-stakes environments across industrial, energy, and corporate sectors.

Track 2 — Enterprise
License + Volume Usage
€45K–€480K ARR
Variable per account · Based on data volume + workflow complexity

Enterprise contracts are not flat licenses. Pricing is structured around data volume processed, verification flows executed, and integration depth — not a fixed per-seat fee. This is why enterprise ARR varies significantly per account.

VARS (€480K ARR) processes 270,000 flows/month across petabytes of ITS telemetry. Gestamp (€230K ARR) runs 1,800 monthly reporting cycles. Repsol (€150K ARR) covers 600–900 verifications/month for regulatory reporting. Each contract reflects the operational footprint of the deployment.

Land & Expand: initial entry targets one critical workflow. Expansion is driven by usage, additional datasets, and module adoption — not by upselling.

Mission & Vision
Mission
To make information defensible — by turning data into structured, traceable, and explainable evidence that organizations can stand behind.
Narratio exists because the inability to explain how information was built is becoming a systemic liability. Regulatory pressure, public scrutiny, and reputational risk have created a structural gap between how organizations produce information and how they must defend it. We close that gap.
Vision
To become the standard trust infrastructure layer for enterprise information — the same way AWS became the standard for compute and Okta for identity.
Narratio is not a vertical tool. It is a horizontal layer that integrates into any stack, any workflow, any sector. Every organization that produces, uses, or publishes information it must defend is a potential user. The category does not yet have an infrastructure owner — we intend to be it.
Strategic Thesis
"The cost of not being able to explain information is becoming higher than the cost of the platform."
Narratio competes when this threshold is crossed. EU AI Act, Digital Services Act, ESG reporting obligations, and the mainstreaming of C2PA content provenance standards are systematically raising this threshold across every sector we target. We do not sell productivity. We sell defensibility — and the market for it is expanding by regulatory mandate.
